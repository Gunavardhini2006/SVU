Sri Venkateswara University

Central Library

The Central Library of Sri Venkateswara University in Tirupati was established in 1955 and has grown from an initial collection of 6,700 books to over 375,600 documents, including books, journals, reports, and digital content.

Key Features:

Vast Collection: Textbooks, reference books, gift books, reports, and back volumes of journals.

Print Journals: Subscribes to approximately 400 national and international journals.

Digital Library: Offers online access to theses, dissertations, and other digital resources.

Services: Circulation, book bank, photocopying, and user awareness programs.

Seating Capacity: Accommodates up to 400 readers across four reading halls.

History & Infrastructure:

Established: Initially housed in the college building, moved to the current facility in 1964.

Library Committee: A dedicated committee advises on operations and development.

Location: Centrally situated on the SVU campus in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.
