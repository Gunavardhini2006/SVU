Sri Venkateswara University

Administrative Building

The Administrative Building of Sri Venkateswara University in Tirupati is a central part of the university’s infrastructure, facilitating essential governance and student support. Located at the heart of the 1000-acre lush campus, it reflects the university’s legacy and administrative efficiency.

Key Features and Functions:

Central Hub: Houses offices for academic planning, finance, examinations, and student welfare.

Modern Amenities: Equipped with digital infrastructure, internet facilities, and meeting rooms.

Strategic Location: Located centrally for easy access by students and staff across departments.

About SVU:

Founded: 1954, under the vision to serve the Rayalaseema region.

Campus: Spread across land leased by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams.

NAAC Accredited: Graded 'A+' for excellence in higher education.
